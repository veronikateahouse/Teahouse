ABOUT US

This website is a final project for the subject of Internet Services and Business Applications, which is an elective course at Matej Bel University in Slovakia. The objective of this course is connected to the economics and as such, this website should have an economic purpose: it sells tea. I have decided on the topic of tea because of personal determination to own a teahouse in a future. The whole website is created in a simplistic way in order to be easily navigated and customers could find tea they want in a fast way and will not be carried away by details. Nevertheless, sales are not the only purpose with which I have tried to work, the other one is a promotion of a teahouse and therefore, the website also tries to increase customer's desire to drink tea and show a serenity that the teahouse offers.
